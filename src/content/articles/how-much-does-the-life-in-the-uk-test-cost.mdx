## What Is the Standard £50 Fee?

The £50 fee applies when you book:
- A slot between **10am and 4pm**
- On a **Monday to Friday**
- With **more than 48 hours notice**

If all three conditions are met, you pay £50. If any condition is not met, an additional fee applies.

---

## When Does the £25 Surcharge Apply?

An additional £25 is added — bringing the total to **£75** — when you book:

- **Outside core hours** — any slot before 10am or after 4pm, any day
- **On a weekend** — Saturday or Sunday slots fall outside core hours
- **At short notice** — booking between 48 and 24 hours before your appointment

Both conditions independently trigger the £25 surcharge. If you book a weekend slot with less than 48 hours notice, you still pay £75 (the surcharges do not stack).

---

## What Is Super Priority (£80)?

Super Priority is the option to sit the test within 24 hours of booking. This adds **£30** to the standard fee, bringing the total to **£80**.

This option exists for people with urgent visa or immigration deadlines. Under normal circumstances there is no reason to pay the Super Priority fee — booking a standard weekday slot at £50 is the same test with the same result.

---

## What Does the Fee Cover?

The fee covers sitting the test at an approved test centre. It includes:

- Your seat at the test centre
- The 24-question computer-based test
- Your result, issued immediately on screen
- Your pass notification letter (if you pass)

It does **not** include the official handbook. The Life in the United Kingdom handbook (3rd edition) costs around £13 and must be purchased or borrowed separately — every test question comes from it.

---

## What Happens if You Fail?

If you fail, you pay the applicable fee again for each retake — there is no discount and no limit on attempts. You can rebook as soon as a slot is available. See the guide on [what happens if you fail the Life in the UK test](/articles/what-happens-if-you-fail-the-life-in-the-uk-test) for the full process.

The pass rate is around 67%. Someone who passes on the second attempt and uses standard slots would pay £100 total.

---

## Is the Fee Refundable?

Yes — but only if you cancel more than 3 days before your test. Cancel within 3 days and you forfeit the fee. You can reschedule for free up to 3 days before your test date.

The fee is not refunded if you fail.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How much does the Life in the UK test cost?

£50 for a standard slot (10am–4pm, Monday to Friday, 48+ hours notice). Out-of-hours or short-notice bookings (48–24 hours) cost £75. Super Priority bookings within 24 hours cost £80.

### Does the Life in the UK test cost more on weekends?

Yes. Weekend slots fall outside the core hours (10am–4pm, Monday to Friday) and incur a £25 surcharge, making the total £75 instead of £50.

### What counts as out of hours for the Life in the UK test?

Any slot outside 10am–4pm Monday to Friday is considered out of hours. This includes early morning slots, evening slots, and all weekend appointments. Out-of-hours bookings cost £75.

### Is there a short-notice booking fee?

Yes. Booking between 48 and 24 hours before your test adds £25, bringing the total to £75. Booking within 24 hours (Super Priority) costs £80.

### Has the Life in the UK test fee increased?

The standard £50 fee has been in place since 2012. The out-of-hours and Super Priority surcharges are additional fees set by the Home Office for non-standard booking times.

### Is there a discount for the Life in the UK test?

No. There are no discounts, concessions, or exemptions from the fee. The applicable fee applies to everyone regardless of age, income, or number of previous attempts.

### Can I pay at the test centre on the day?

No. Payment is made online when you book through the official GOV.UK booking system. You cannot pay on the day at the test centre.

### What is the total cost if I fail twice?

Three standard attempts cost £150 (3 × £50). This is why thorough preparation matters — use [free practice questions](/practice) to make sure you are consistently scoring above 75% before you book.

## Expert Tips

**1. Book a standard weekday slot to pay £50.**
Avoid early morning, evening, and weekend slots unless you have no alternative. Choosing a slot between 10am and 4pm on a weekday with more than 48 hours notice means you pay the minimum fee.

**2. Do not book at short notice.**
Booking within 48 hours of your test adds £25 with no benefit — it is the exact same test. Only use Super Priority if you genuinely have an urgent deadline.

**3. Book only when you are consistently scoring 90%+ in practice.**
The fee is non-refundable. There is no financial benefit to booking early if you are not ready. See our [booking guide](/articles/how-to-book-the-life-in-the-uk-test) for when and how to book.

**4. Budget for the handbook too.**
The official handbook costs around £13. Your total outlay for a single standard attempt is closer to £63 if you buy the book new.
